  • Does Payro cover other expenses other than payroll?

    No, Payro is a unique loan just to help businesses cover payroll. (Payro isn’t available for payments to freelancers or outside contractors who aren’t paid through payroll).

    This guarantees you funds for payroll that are available on standby, while keeping the process fast and easy compared to other loan & lines of credit alternatives.

  • How do I request financing?

    Apply in minutes through our app here.

    After your initial approval, we’ll let you know how much you’re approved for, and you can request funds anytime through the Payro app.

  • What if my payroll is more than the amount approved?

    You can get funded up to your maximum approval.

    You can also always apply for an increase.
    Our underwriters will review your financials, re-evaluate, and let you know if you’re eligible for more funding.

  • Will applying to Payro impact my credit?

    No, applying won’t impact your credit score.
